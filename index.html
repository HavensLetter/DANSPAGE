About Dr. Daniel Ivankovich

Dr. Daniel Ivankovich is an internationally recognized orthopedic surgeon dedicated to restoring mobility and dignity to patients suffering from knee and hip conditions. Known as "The Bone Healer," he blends expert surgical precision with a deep commitment to underserved communities.

With over two decades of experience, Dr. Dan has performed thousands of successful joint replacement surgeries and has become a leading advocate for patient-first care across all demographics.
