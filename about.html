Meet Dr. Daniel Ivankovich

Orthopedic Surgeon • Advocate • Bone Healer

Restoring More Than Bones

Dr. Daniel Ivankovich is more than an orthopedic surgeon — he’s a movement maker. Known nationally as “The Bone Healer,” he has dedicated his life to restoring mobility, dignity, and hope to thousands of patients in underserved communities.

A Surgeon with Soul

With over two decades of experience specializing in spine, knee, and hip reconstruction, Dr. Ivankovich brings both clinical precision and deep compassion to every case. His philosophy is simple: every patient deserves expert care, regardless of income, background, or insurance status.

Bridging Medicine and Mission

As co-founder of the OnePatient Global Health Initiative, Dr. Ivankovich has delivered over $50 million in free surgical and rehabilitative services to Chicago-area patients who would have otherwise gone untreated. His work has been featured by CNN Heroes, Oprah, NPR, and countless other outlets.

Beyond the Operating Room

A former blues musician and community activist, Dr. Dan’s presence extends beyond the hospital. He’s as comfortable with a guitar as he is with a scalpel — bringing healing and rhythm to every space he enters.

Credentials That Matter

  • Board-Certified Orthopedic Surgeon
  • Fellowship-Trained in Spine and Joint Reconstruction
  • Medical Director, OnePatient Global Health
  • Recognized by CNN Heroes, Chicago Tribune, and Rolling Stone

Why Patients Trust Dr. Dan

Dr. Ivankovich doesn’t just perform procedures — he changes lives. From chronic pain relief to total joint renewal, his outcomes speak for themselves. Patients trust him not just for his skill, but for his genuine care.
